About Click-Dish

Welcome to Click-Dish, your AI-powered culinary assistant! This app is designed to make finding, creating, and saving recipes effortless.

Search for delicious recipes by ingredients or dish name, with results instantly tailored to your dietary restrictions set in your Profile.

Use the + tab to Generate unique recipes from scratch or by uploading a photo of ingredients you have on hand.

Dont't forget to visit Saved to keep track of all your favorite finds!

With Click-Dish you'll no longer be answering, "What's for Dinner?"

Our Core Features

AI Recipe Generation Use the latest Gemini models to instantly create new recipes from text or images.

Personalized Search Get instant, custom recipe results filtered by your saved dietary restrictions.

Save & Organize Keep all your discovered and generated recipes in one easy-to-access Saved section.

The Search feature uses the Gemini API to find 4 recipes based on your query, applying your saved Dietary Restrictions automatically for a curated result list.
